FAQ

What types of services do you offer?

Server Setups, Plugin Configurations, Tebex or Webstores Setups, Discord Server, Resource Pack (Oraxen, ItemsAdder or Vanilla Resourcepack), Models Setups, Voting System, Custom Plugin and many more, just inquire.

What are your prices?

My prices are negotiable and depend on the project's scope and complexity. Please contact me to discuss your requirements and get a personalized quote.

How long does a typical project take?

Project times really depend on what you need. Simple tasks can be done pretty quickly, while bigger setups or custom development might take a bit longer. I always aim to deliver on time and keep things moving.

Can you develop custom Java plugins?

Yes, with 2 years of experience developing custom Java plugins for Minecraft servers. Whether it's a simple feature or a more complex system, I make sure the plugin works smoothly and fits your server's needs.

Do you configure ItemsAdder or Oraxen?

Yes! I offer setup and configuration services for both ItemsAdder and Oraxen to add custom items, textures, models, and mechanics to your server.
